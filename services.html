Services & Technical Support

We deliver end-to-end renewable energy services — from engineering design and procurement to installation, training and long-term operations. Our approach prioritizes community ownership, technical robustness and measurable impact.

Request a Consultation

Core Services

Our services are modular—pick what you need. We scope projects to local loads, site resources and community goals, then design resilient, maintainable systems that last.

System Design & Sizing

Load assessments, PV & battery sizing (kWp / kWh), BOS specification, and monitoring architecture. We deliver technical drawings, BOMs and procurement-ready documentation.

Deliverables: Site survey, sizing report, equipment spec, single-line diagrams.

Installation & Commissioning

Professional installation of PV arrays, inverters, controllers and batteries; commissioning, testing and performance verification to ensure systems meet specification.

Includes: Mechanical, electrical, safety checks, and commissioning reports.

Monitoring, O&M & Remote Support

Real-time monitoring, periodic maintenance, and rapid remote diagnostics keep uptime high. We set up dashboards and alerting for critical assets.

Benefits: Reduced downtime, extended asset life, better reporting for funders.

Training & Local Capacity Building

Hands-on technician courses, management training for co-ops, and community sessions on energy efficiency and safety to enable local ownership.

Format: Practical workshops, train-the-trainer models, and O&M manuals.

Financing & Grants Support

We help craft funding proposals, run cost-benefit analyses, and connect projects to grant programs and impact investors to scale solutions.

Community Engagement & Impact Measurement

Participatory planning, stakeholder workshops, and monitoring of KPI's (uptime, kWh delivered, user-reported outcomes) to ensure equitable, measurable impact.

Need a tailored package? Contact us and we’ll scope a solution with your community and funders in mind.

Service Packages & Next Steps

  • Starter: Site survey, basic PV system design, and installation support (ideal for small clinics & homes).
  • Standard: Full design, installation, commissioning and 12 months monitoring & maintenance.
  • Comprehensive: Full project delivery including financing support, local training and 3-year O&M package.
